Frequently Asked Questions

What experience do I need?

Experience requirements vary by expedition. We offer trips for all levels, from beginner treks to expert technical climbs.

What's included in the price?

Prices include guide services, group equipment, permits, base camp meals, and in-country transport. Flights and personal gear are separate.

How do I prepare physically?

We provide detailed training plans 3-6 months before your expedition. Focus on cardio, strength, and acclimatization hikes.

What about insurance?

We require comprehensive travel and evacuation insurance. We can recommend providers and verify coverage before departure.
