About Mountain Expedition

Founded in 2005, Mountain Expedition began with a simple mission: to share our passion for mountains with adventurers worldwide while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

What started as a small team of three experienced climbers has grown into a premier mountain expedition service with over 50 certified guides and partnerships across the globe's most iconic peaks.

We've successfully guided over 1,000 climbers to summits, from beginners taking their first steps on a glacier to experienced mountaineers tackling 8,000-meter peaks. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every journey is not just a climb, but a life-changing experience.

We are dedicated to sustainable tourism and empowering local communities, ensuring that our presence leaves a positive impact on both the environment and the people we trek with.
