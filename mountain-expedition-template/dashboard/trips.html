Trip Dossier

Detailed itinerary and information for your expedition

Everest Base Camp Trek

May 15 - May 28, 2025 • 14 Days

Moderate Difficulty Max Altitude: 5,364m Group Size: 8-12

Trip Highlights

  • ✓ Reach Everest Base Camp at 5,364m
  • ✓ Visit Kala Patthar viewpoint (5,545m)
  • ✓ Experience Sherpa culture in Namche Bazaar
  • ✓ Explore Tengboche Monastery
  • ✓ Stunning Himalayan mountain views

What's Included

  • ✓ Experienced guide & porters
  • ✓ All meals during trek
  • ✓ Teahouse accommodations
  • ✓ Permits & national park fees
  • ✓ Emergency evacuation insurance

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Transfer to hotel. Welcome dinner and expedition briefing with your guide team.

Day 2: Fly to Lukla, Trek to Phakding (2,610m)

Scenic flight to Lukla. Begin trek through pine forests to Phakding. 3-4 hours hiking.

Day 3-4: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

Climb to the Sherpa capital. Acclimatization day with optional hikes to Everest View Hotel.

Day 5-7: Trek to Dingboche via Tengboche

Visit famous monastery. Continue through rhododendron forests to Dingboche for acclimatization.

Day 8-9: Trek to Everest Base Camp (5,364m)

Reach Gorak Shep, then continue to Base Camp. Return to Gorak Shep for overnight.

Day 10: Climb Kala Patthar, Descend

Early morning climb for sunrise views of Everest. Begin descent to Pheriche.

Day 11-13: Return Trek to Lukla

Descend through familiar villages. Celebrate completion in Lukla.

Day 14: Fly to Kathmandu, Departure

Morning flight to Kathmandu. Transfer to airport for international departure.

Important Notes

Physical Fitness

Good cardiovascular fitness required. Able to walk 5-6 hours per day with a daypack.

Altitude

Proper acclimatization is built into itinerary. Follow guide instructions carefully.

Weather

Spring (Mar-May) and autumn (Sep-Nov) are best seasons. Prepare for cold temperatures.

Communication

WiFi available at most teahouses. Satellite phone for emergencies.
